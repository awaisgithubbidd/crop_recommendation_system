Upload Dataset for Batch Analysis

Upload a CSV file containing soil and climate parameters for multiple samples to get batch crop recommendations.

Required CSV Format

Your CSV file must include the following columns:

  • N - Nitrogen content (kg/yards)
  • P - Phosphorous content (kg/yards)
  • K - Potassium content (kg/yards)
  • temperature - Temperature in degrees Celsius
  • humidity - Relative humidity percentage
  • ph - pH value of the soil
  • rainfall - Rainfall in mm

View sample CSV format

Benefits of Batch Analysis

Efficient

Process multiple soil samples at once, saving time and effort.

Comprehensive

Get a summary analysis of all your data, including visualization of crop distribution.

Exportable

Receive results in a downloadable CSV format for further analysis.

Area-Based

Ideal for analyzing multiple locations or different areas within the same farm.
